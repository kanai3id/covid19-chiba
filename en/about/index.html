About this site

This site was opened unofficially in Chiba Prefecture to provide the latest information on the new coronavirus infection (COVID-19).

I use mechanism of Tokyo Official New Coronavirus Countermeasures Site , which is released under an open source license that allows copying and modification.

By communicating official information and objective figures from Chiba prefecture in an easy-to-understand manner, it is appropriate for those who live in Chiba prefecture, those who have bases in Chiba prefecture, and those who visit Chiba prefecture to understand the current situation. The purpose is to be able to take various measures.

About the data

The data is updated by acquiring the data from the site in Chiba prefecture The data currently used are as follows
  • Information on new coronavirus infections in Chiba Prefecture
    • Details of people testing positive
    • Status of residential area
    • Number of confirmed cases
    • PCR and antigen test status
    • Number of positive patients by age
